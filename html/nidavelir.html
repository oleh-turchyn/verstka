Monterosa Freeride Paradise

Champoluc - Frachey, Monterosa, Italy
  • Italy
  • 5 days tour
  • up to 4 people
  • 2 tour gides
  • Sleep in hotel
  • Difficulty: medium
  • 499$

Monterosa Freeride Paradise is a secret you want to discover. Freeriding Paradise is the official name of the huge ski area that combines three resorts: Champoluc - Frachey, Gressoney la Trinite - Stafal and Alagna - Valsesia, on either side of two ridges adjacent to the Monte Rosa Massif, the second highest in The Alps. Differences in altitudes from 1220 to 3275, glaciers, fields, slopes and forests, plus 180 kilometers of well maintained trails, because we are in Italy. The place is ethnographic. Bear angle, patriarchal villages, friendly local people. End of January in Monte Rosa - low season, few people and skip almost for nothing. And the time is snowy! We invite skiers and snowboarders, both average and advanced.

Trekking in Georgia

Tusheti and Khevsureti, Georgia
  • Georgia
  • 5 days tour
  • up to 4 people
  • 2 tour gides
  • Sleep in private tents
  • Difficulty: medium
  • 399$

This hike will show you the pristine, "non-tourist" Georgia that it was centuries ago ... You will see the majestic Kazbek, swim in the high mountains of Abudelaur, walk around the medieval fortress cities and abandoned villages. Paganism prevailed here - until the Soviet era!
